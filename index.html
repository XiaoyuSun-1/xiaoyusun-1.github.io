Research & Projects

Biomedical Imaging

At Washington University in St. Louis, I compared foundation and task-specific models for retinal OCT fluid segmentation, developed Python pipelines for preprocessing and training, and worked on fluid quantification for treatment-effect assessment.

Computational Neuroscience

My work in computational neuroscience includes EEG motor-imagery decoding with complex-valued methods, adaptation and evaluation of TIMBRE and cCSP, and analysis of pupil dynamics in learning and game performance.

Clinical Software Design

Through Project Femto, I designed an interactive Qt interface for femtosecond laser planning with synchronized parameter editing, procedure-specific tools, clinician feedback, and export workflows for surgical planning.
